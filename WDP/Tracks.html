About BSIT

The Information technology major is designed to offer pathways to highly-demanded careers in IT. The IT major is project based. Students will learn how to use IT to solve real-world problems within the context of a field in which they wish to work in. In this program, graduates will be familiar with, and be able to apply, the following core information technologies to solve business problems: programming, networking, databases, and web technologies.
